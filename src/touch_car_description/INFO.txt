Package: touch_car_description

What it is for:
Robot description package for a small three-wheel car. The car is about 15 cm
long, has two motorized rear wheels, one passive caster wheel, and a front
touch bumper contact sensor for Gazebo.

Useful commands:
  source /opt/ros/jazzy/setup.bash
  colcon build --packages-select touch_car_description
  ros2 launch touch_car_gazebo room.launch.py
