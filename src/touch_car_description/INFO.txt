Package: touch_car_description

What it is for:
Robot description package for a small three-wheel car. The car has two
motorized rear wheels, one passive caster wheel, a front bumper, and a front
camera model. The launched Gazebo SDF includes the active 650 x 900 front camera
sensor.

Useful commands:
  source /opt/ros/jazzy/setup.bash
  colcon build --packages-select touch_car_description
  source install/setup.bash
  ros2 launch touch_car_gazebo room.launch.py
